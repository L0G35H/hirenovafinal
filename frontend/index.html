Platform Features

Everything You Need to
Hire Better, Faster

Smart PDF Upload

Drag-and-drop PDF uploads with automatic text extraction. Supports both digital PDFs and scanned/image-based resumes.

OCR Fallback

Automatically detects image-based PDFs and switches to Tesseract OCR for accurate text extraction — no manual intervention needed.

AI Skill Detection

Extracts 100+ skills across 8 categories: Programming, Web, Frameworks, DevOps, Databases, Design, Data Science, and Soft Skills.

Smart Scoring

Calculates a match score based on required vs. detected skills. Domain mismatch results in a zero score — no false positives.

Candidate Rankings

Auto-ranks all candidates by score with filterable leaderboards by skill, score range, job role, and education level.

Analytics Dashboard

Visual analytics with Chart.js: skill frequency bars, score distribution, top candidates, and upload trends.

The Process

How TalentLens Works

1

Upload & Extract

Candidate uploads PDF resume. System extracts text via pdfplumber or Tesseract OCR.

2

Detect & Match

AI engine detects all skills and compares with admin-defined required skills for the role.

3

Score & Rank

Score is calculated, candidate stored in Firebase, and ranked on the admin dashboard.

About the Project

Built for KGiSL Institute of Technology's Placement Cell

TalentLens was designed to solve the real-world problem of manual resume screening in college placement drives. The KGiSL Placement Cell processes hundreds of student resumes every academic year, and this platform automates the entire pipeline.

By leveraging AI-powered skill extraction and smart scoring algorithms, placement coordinators can now identify the most qualified candidates in seconds instead of days.
